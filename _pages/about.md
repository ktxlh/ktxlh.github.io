I am a final-year double major in Computer Science and Data Science and Technology at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology](https://hkust.edu.hk/) with Full Admission Scholarship. My research interests include representation learning and natural language understanding.

My interst in NLP began when I did a project regarding text classification under [Prof. Hsin-Hsi Chen](http://nlg.csie.ntu.edu.tw/groupmember.php)'s supervision in [National Taiwan University](https://www.ntu.edu.tw/english/). Afterwards, I followed my interest to research-intern in [Academia Sinica](iis.sinica.edu.tw/zh/index.html) under [Prof. Keh-Yih Su](https://homepage.iis.sinica.edu.tw/pages/kysu/)'s supervision, focusing on text generation with Transformer-based models. Currently, I am working on two more projcets in graph neural networks and session recommendation with [Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing Wong](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~raywong/).

[Here](https://ktxlh.github.io/files/Resume_Shang_Ling_Hsu.pdf) is my 1-page resume.

When I am not writing code, I sing a cappella, jazz dance, and promote Women in Tech.
